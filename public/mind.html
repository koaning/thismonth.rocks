Pomodoro

Pomodoro is a method of working in 25 minute segments.


It makes you aware of the time in a day and it prevents you from getting stuck in a rabbit hole. There's a great video on youtube.


There's great apps that you can download but the most popular one seems to be tomighty. If you're interested in knowing more behind the theory behind it you might appreciate reading the book written by the creator.


If possible, consider buying the book from a local book-store.

Gift a Stranger

The largest gift exchange on the planet is reddit gifts. They offer all sorts of categories for gift giving and they have a match-making process. You can send a gift to Slovenia and get one from Africa.

There's some gems that were given too. There's that time when Arnold Schwarzenegger drew a cat and that time when somebody sent a fully functional safe.

The best advice here is go to overboard with decorations. It's a great feeling to make somebody feel special. Especially if the whole world is doing it.
